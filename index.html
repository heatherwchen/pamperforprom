Fashion is Empowering

We believe that style and fashion can help build confidence in teens. That's why Pamper for Prom is working with Wish Upon A Teen to help make sure that every teen at the Evening of Dreams Prom looks good and feels great.

Evening of Dreams Prom

On May 18, Wish Upon A Teen will host their annual Evening of Dreams Prom, which allows teens with severe life-limiting medical conditions to attend prom.

Clothing Drives

From February 4 to March 29, Pampering for Prom will be collecting new and lightly used clothing items for the fashion show!

Fashion Show

All contributions from our drives will be modeled at our fashion show! Teens will be able to see all the apparel options they can pick from during the runway show, and take an outfit home.

Spa Day

We will be running a spa day in conjunction with Wish Upon A Teen. Through spa-services, teens can relax and get ready for the prom.

Help Change A Life

Running events can add up to daunting costs. You can support us by contributing to our GoFundMe, and help make a teen's dream night come true. We also are accepting dresses, suits, shoes, and ties. Please contact us if you have items you would like to donate.
